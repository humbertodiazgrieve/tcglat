Pokémon Trading Card Game is one of the most popular collectible card games in the world. If you have always wanted to play but did not know where to start, this guide is for you.

## Why Pokémon TCG?

Pokémon TCG offers:

- **Active community**: Millions of players worldwide
- **Accessibility**: Starter decks from $15 USD
- **Depth**: Complex strategy for competitive players
- **Nostalgia**: For lifelong fans

## Step 1: Choose Your Format

Before buying cards, decide which format you are going to play:

### Standard
- Cards from the last 2-3 years
- More dynamic meta
- Requires frequent updates

### Expanded
- Cards from 2012 onward
- More strategic options
- Higher initial cost

### Casual (Pokécollector)
- Casual format
- Ideal for playing with friends

## Step 2: Get Your First Deck

### Budget option ($15-25 USD)
Buy an official Battle Deck or Theme Deck. They include 60 ready-to-play cards.

### Recommended option ($25-40 USD)
Buy a Starter Set + a Structure Deck of the same type. Combine them for a more competitive deck.

## Step 3: Learn the Basic Rules

1. **Objective**: Knock out two of your opponent's Pokémon
2. **Energy**: You need specific energy to use attacks
3. **Evolution**: Evolve your Pokémon to become stronger

## Step 4: Join the Community

- Look for local card stores
- Join Facebook/Discord groups
- Attend local tournaments (Prerelease events)

## Conclusion

Pokémon TCG is an accessible and fun game. With this guide you now have the basics to start. Good luck on your Pokémon adventure!
