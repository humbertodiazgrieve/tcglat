Disney Lorcana has not only come to stay, but it does so with an ambitious expansion plan. Since its launch, each new set has expanded the universe of characters, mechanics, and strategies. If you are a collector or player, knowing the history of the expansions helps you understand the evolution of the game and anticipate what is coming. In this article we review the first Lorcana expansions and their most important news.

## The launch of Lorcana

Disney Lorcana was announced in 2022 and officially launched in 2023. From the very first moment, the combination of Disney and card games generated enormous expectation. The first set introduced the six colors of the game, iconic characters, and the basic mechanics that define Lorcana.

The reception was so positive that products sold out quickly in many stores, making Lorcana one of the most successful launches of the year in the TCG world.

## The First Chapter

**The First Chapter** is Lorcana's inaugural set. It was responsible for introducing the game to the world and laid the foundations for everything that would come after. It included characters such as Mickey Mouse, Ariel, Belle, Elsa, Aladdin, Peter Pan, Scar, Gaston, and many more.

**Highlights:**
- Introduction of the six Lorcana colors.
- Staple cards that remain relevant in the metagame.
- High-quality art with illustrations of Disney characters.
- Different rarities: common, uncommon, rare, super rare, legendary, and enchanted.

This set was the starting point for thousands of players and collectors who joined the Lorcana universe.

## Rise of the Floodborn

**Rise of the Floodborn** expanded the game with new characters, mechanics, and strategies. The theme explored the concept of "floodborn" characters, transformed versions of iconic Disney figures. This allowed designers to create cards with dual faces and more complex abilities.

**News:**
- Floodborn characters with alternate versions.
- New synergies between colors.
- More enchanted and collectible cards.
- Expansion of the game's narrative.

This set consolidated Lorcana as a game with real depth and not just a nostalgia product.

## Into the Inklands

**Into the Inklands** took players to explore new territories within the world of Lorcana. The theme focused on locations, adventures, and characters related to travel and discoveries. It added more location cards and mechanics that reward exploration.

**Highlights:**
- Location cards with lasting effects.
- More characters from diverse Disney franchises.
- New songs and thematic actions.
- Continuation of the game's story.

This set showed that Lorcana could continue innovating while keeping its accessible essence.

## Ursula's Return

**Ursula's Return** put the focus on one of Disney's most iconic villains: Ursula. The expansion explored the game's plot with the villain as the central antagonist, adding characters and cards related to the ocean, magic, and manipulation.

**News:**
- Ursula as the central figure of the narrative.
- New villain cards with disruptive abilities.
- More options for control and aggression decks.
- Thematic art inspired by the sea and fantasy.

## Impact on the metagame

Each expansion has affected the Lorcana metagame. New cards have redefined which colors are competitive, which archetypes work, and how decks are built. Players must constantly adapt to new mechanics and synergies.

## How to collect expansions

If you collect Lorcana, there are several ways to approach expansions:

- **Complete the set**: get every card in an expansion.
- **Collect by franchise**: focus on characters from your favorite movies.
- **Foil and enchanted**: look for the special versions of cards.
- **By utility**: buy only the cards you use in your decks.

## Conclusion

The first Disney Lorcana expansions have laid the foundations for a game with a bright future. Each set has expanded the universe, added beloved characters, and deepened the mechanics. If you are starting out, knowing the history of these sets helps you understand the present of the game and make better collection decisions.
