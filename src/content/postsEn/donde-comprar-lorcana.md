Disney Lorcana has generated enormous excitement since its announcement, and products usually sell out quickly. If you want to start collecting or playing, it is important to know where to buy original products at a good price. In this guide we show you the best options for buying Lorcana.

## Local specialty stores

Board game and card stores are the best starting point. Many of them are authorized distributors of Ravensburger, the company behind Lorcana. In addition to selling products, they usually organize events, tournaments, and game nights.

**Advantages of buying in a physical store:**
- You can see the product before buying.
- You receive personalized advice.
- You participate in community events.
- You support local commerce.

Ask at your usual store about the availability of starter decks, booster packs, and booster boxes. Demand is high, so it is worth staying alert for releases.

## Online stores and marketplaces

On the internet you will find several specialty stores and marketplaces that sell Lorcana products. Popular options include board game stores with international shipping, Amazon, eBay, and local platforms such as Mercado Libre.

**Advantages:**
- Greater variety and access to products sold out in physical stores.
- Possibility to compare prices between sellers.
- Home delivery.

**Disadvantages:**
- You cannot inspect the product before receiving it.
- Shipping costs and possible customs issues.
- Risk of unauthorized sellers.

If you buy online, choose sellers with good reputation and avoid suspiciously low prices.

## Lorcana products available

The main Lorcana products include:

- **Starter Decks**: preconstructed decks ready to play. Ideal for beginners.
- **Booster Packs**: packs with 12 cards. Perfect for expanding your collection.
- **Gift Sets**: special sets with promotional cards and accessories.
- **Illumineer's Trove**: collection boxes with boosters, counters, and accessories.
- **Bundles and special editions**: commemorative and limited products.

## Singles and loose cards

As Lorcana grows, so does the singles market. Buying loose cards is the most efficient way to complete a specific deck without depending on the luck of packs. Platforms like TCGplayer, Cardmarket, and buy-sell groups usually have offers.

**Tips for buying singles:**
- Ask for photos of the card, especially if it is high rarity.
- Verify condition and authenticity.
- Compare prices between several sellers.
- Use payment methods with buyer protection.

## How to avoid fraud

The popularity of Lorcana has also attracted unreliable sellers. To avoid fraud:

- Buy at stores and authorized distributors.
- Be wary of sealed products at prices well below market value.
- Verify that the packaging has not been altered.
- For expensive cards, ask for authentication or buy from sellers with references.

## Conclusion

Buying Disney Lorcana can be a challenge due to high demand, but with patience and good sources you can get original products. Whether you prefer the experience of a local store, the convenience of buying online, or the hunt for deals, the important thing is to research and compare before buying.
