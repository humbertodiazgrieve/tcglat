The Pokémon TCG meta continues to evolve. In this article we analyze the strongest competitive decks of the moment.

## Top 5 Meta Decks

### 1. Charizard ex / Palkia ex

The most consistent deck of the format. It offers a perfect balance between speed and power.

**Key cards:**
- Charizard ex (Obsidian Flames)
- Palkia ex (Stellar Crown)
- Boss's Orders

### 2. Miraidon ex

An Electric control deck that has gained popularity. It uses fixed damage and recovery.

### 3. Lugia ex / Archeops

The most aggressive deck of the format. It can deal 280 damage points in a single turn.

### 4. Roaring Moon ex

A Dragon deck with excellent synergy. Strong against the current meta.

### 5. Gyarados ex

A Water control deck. Excellent against aggressive decks.

## Matchup Analysis

| Deck | vs Charizard | vs Miraidon | vs Lugia |
|------|--------------|-------------|----------|
| Charizard | - | 55-45 | 45-55 |
| Miraidon | 45-55 | - | 50-50 |
| Lugia | 55-45 | 50-50 | - |

## Recommendations for Climbers

1. **Practice with a single deck**: Master one before trying others
2. **Know the matchups**: Learn how to play against each archetype
3. **Do not be afraid to lose**: Consistency comes with practice

## Conclusion

The meta is balanced. Any of the top 5 is viable for competitive play. Choose the one that best suits your style!
