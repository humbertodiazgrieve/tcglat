In Disney Lorcana, not all cards have the same impact. Some become pillars of almost any deck, while others will only shine in very specific strategies. If you want to improve your builds or invest in valuable cards, knowing the best Lorcana cards is essential. In this article we review staples by color and especially sought-after cards.

## What is a staple in Lorcana?

A **staple** is a card so versatile and powerful that it works in many different decks. It does not depend on a specific synergy, but provides value on its own. Having staples in your collection allows you to build multiple decks without depending on niche cards.

## Amber staples

Amber focuses on support and protection. Its best cards usually heal characters, generate lore safely, or empower allies.

- **Ariel - On Her Own**: a character with good lore generation and resistance.
- **Belle - Strange but Special**: useful for value strategies and hand control.
- **Healing Touch**: a classic healing action to keep characters on the board.
- **The Queen - Wicked and Vain**: provides presence and board control.

These cards are excellent for support and midrange decks.

## Amethyst staples

Amethyst shines in control and manipulation. Its key cards allow you to draw, return cards, and control the pace.

- **Mickey Mouse - Wayward Sorcerer**: one of the most iconic and versatile cards.
- **Maleficent - Sorceress**: excellent for control and removal.
- **Be Prepared**: a powerful action to clear the opponent's threats.
- **Let the Storm Rage On**: useful for controlling the board and generating advantage.

Amethyst is usually the favorite color of control players.

## Emerald staples

Emerald stands out for evasion, combos, and clever play. Its best cards surprise the opponent and generate unexpected advantage.

- **Aladdin - Street Rat**: aggressive and difficult to block.
- **Peter Pan - Never Landing**: excellent for evasion strategies.
- **Stolen Scimitar**: a useful item for Emerald characters.
- **You Have Forgotten Me**: removal with advantageous conditions.

Emerald is perfect for players who enjoy cunning.

## Ruby staples

Ruby is pure aggression. Its cards seek to challenge, damage, and close games quickly.

- **Gaston - Arrogant Hunter**: a character with great strength and pressure.
- **Scar - Vicious Cheater**: ideal for challenges and aggressive control.
- **Tamatoa - So Shiny!**: combines value and damage.
- **Dragon Fire**: a highly sought-after direct removal action.

Ruby is the color for those who want to win quickly.

## Sapphire staples

Sapphire specializes in drawing, ink, and resource control. Its cards ensure you never run out of options.

- **Elsa - Snow Queen**: controls the board and generates advantage.
- **Merlin - Turtle**: excellent for card draw and value strategies.
- **A Whole New World**: an iconic song to refill your hand.
- **Develop Your Brain**: a key search action for consistency.

Sapphire is ideal for players who prefer resource control.

## Steel staples

Steel is defense and resilience. Its cards build walls that the opponent can hardly break.

- **Beast - Tragic Hero**: a character with enormous resistance.
- **Hercules - True Hero**: perfect for defending and challenging.
- **Shield of Virtue**: a very useful defensive item.
- **Tangle**: an action to control the board.

Steel is for those who prefer to endure and counterattack.

## High collectible value cards

In addition to their utility, some Lorcana cards have great collectible value due to their rarity, illustration, or limited edition. Foil, enchanted, and special promotional cards are usually the most sought after. If you collect, pay attention to the condition and rarity of each card.

## Conclusion

Knowing the staples and the best cards of each color helps you build better decks and understand the metagame. You don't need all the expensive cards to compete, but you do need to know which ones add the most value to your strategy. Over time, you will discover which ones best suit your style.
