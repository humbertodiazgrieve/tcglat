Knowing the best One Piece Card Game cards is essential for building competitive decks and making good purchases. Some cards are so versatile that they appear in multiple strategies, while others will only shine in specific archetypes. In this article we review the most important cards of each color and the staples you should know.

## What is a staple in One Piece Card Game?

A **staple** is a card so good on its own that it is included in many different decks. It does not depend on a complex combination, but provides direct value. Having staples in your collection allows you to build several decks without investing in niche cards.

## Red staples

Red is the color of aggression. Its best cards seek to pressure, attack, and eliminate characters quickly.

- **Luffy - Monkey D. Luffy**: the character version is usually powerful and synergistic with red strategies.
- **Sanji - Black Leg**: excellent for applying pressure and generating damage.
- **Fire Fist**: a highly sought-after direct removal event.
- **Red Hawk**: damage and aggression in a single card.

These cards are common in aggressive red decks and in combinations with other colors.

## Green staples

Green specializes in card draw, healing, and resources. Its key cards keep the hand full and the deck running.

- **Nami - Cat Burglar**: generates resources and allows you to select cards.
- **Zoro - Three Swords Style**: a solid combat character.
- **Gum-Gum Giant Pistol**: versatile event for red and green.
- **Cyclone**: useful control event to manage threats.

Green is ideal for players who enjoy resource advantage.

## Blue staples

Blue is the color of control. Its cards allow you to return, block, and draw, controlling the pace of the game.

- **Law - Trafalgar Law**: character with control and removal effects.
- **Robin - Devil Child**: useful for drawing and managing resources.
- **Shambles**: iconic event to move characters and disorganize the opponent.
- **Counter Shot**: efficient defensive response.

Blue is perfect for players who prefer to think several moves ahead.

## Purple staples

Purple represents brute power and villains. Its cards are usually more expensive but with devastating effects.

- **Kaido - King of the Beasts**: massive character with impact on the field.
- **King - The Wildfire**: high power and presence.
- **Blast Breath**: mass removal event.
- **Thunder Bagua**: powerful attack that can change the game.

Purple is for those who enjoy destroying the opponent's board.

## Black staples

Black focuses on utility, control, and graveyard effects. Its key cards limit the opponent's options.

- **Crocodile - Sir Crocodile**: character with control and manipulation effects.
- **Mr. 1 - Daz Bones**: defense and presence in combat.
- **Desert Spada**: removal and damage in a single event.
- **Poison**: utility effect to control characters.

Black is ideal for control and midrange strategies.

## Important event cards

In addition to characters, events are crucial. Some of the most versatile include:

- **Removal events**: eliminate the opponent's characters.
- **Draw events**: refill your hand and maintain pressure.
- **Defense events**: protect your leader or key characters.
- **Search events**: improve the consistency of your deck.

Including a good variety of events is key to responding to different situations.

## How to invest in cards

If you are starting out, you don't need to buy all the expensive cards right away. Prioritize:

1. Cards that fit your leader and strategy.
2. Staples that work in several decks.
3. Events and characters that cover the deck's needs.
4. High-rarity cards only if you really need them or collect them.

## Conclusion

The best One Piece Card Game cards combine utility, power, and versatility. Knowing the staples by color allows you to build better decks and make better purchasing decisions. Over time, you will discover which cards best suit your playstyle.
